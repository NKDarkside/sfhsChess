About Us

Meeting Information

Everything you need to know about our regular meetings

Regular Meeting Schedule

Days

Every Tuesday & Thursday

Time

3:30 PM - 5:00 PM

Location

School Library

Duration

1.5 Hours per session

What We Do During Meetings

Practice Games

Play casual games with other members to improve your skills and try new strategies.

Strategy Lessons

Learn opening principles, tactical patterns, and endgame techniques from experienced players.

Mini Tournaments

Participate in friendly competitions and prepare for larger tournaments.

Chess Puzzles

Solve tactical puzzles together to sharpen your pattern recognition and calculation skills.

Meeting Guidelines

What to Bring
  • Your enthusiasm for chess
  • Notebook for recording games (optional)
  • Water bottle to stay hydrated
  • Positive attitude and sportsmanship
Club Rules
  • Respect all players and equipment
  • No electronic devices during games
  • Help clean up after meetings
  • Follow school policies at all times

Questions About Meetings?

If you have any questions about our meetings or need more information, don't hesitate to reach out to us.
