Our Mission

Our main goal at South Forsyth Chess Club is to spread chess as a competitive sport and share this game with our community. We also aim to learn and improve as a group.

We hope to compete at the regional, state, and maybe even national level. The club plans to host tournaments at SFHS, and members are encouraged to follow the Instagram page.

Our Goals

Skill Development

Help every member improve their chess skills through structured learning, practice, and mentorship from experienced players.

Community Building

Create a supportive community where students form friendships, learn from each other, and develop social skills.

Competitive Excellence

Prepare members for local and regional tournaments while fostering healthy competition and good sportsmanship.

Critical Thinking

Develop analytical and problem-solving skills that benefit students in academics and life beyond chess.

Our Story

The South Forsyth High School Chess Club was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate chess enthusiasts who wanted to share their love of the game with fellow students. What started as informal lunch meetings has grown into one of the school's most active and successful clubs.

Over the years, our members have competed in numerous regional tournaments, won several awards, and most importantly, formed lasting friendships. We've helped dozens of students discover their passion for chess, from complete beginners to advanced players seeking to refine their skills.

Today, we continue to grow and evolve, always staying true to our core mission of making chess accessible, enjoyable, and educational for all South Forsyth High School students.
